What the Platform Solves

📝

Complete request intake

The form captures title, description, deadline, category, skills, and languages so teams do not lose context.

🔍

Role-based internal processing

Super-admins and company workers see only the requests they are responsible for and can prioritize quickly.

🧠

Expert recommendations

The scoring logic combines skills, languages, profile signals, ratings, and availability to produce a shortlist.

💬

Request and team chat

Communication stays tied to each request so progress, ownership, and blockers remain easy to track.

📊

Transparent work status

The dashboard shows open requests, assignments, available experts, and completed cases in one view.

🏅

Requester-verified completion

When help is done, the requester confirms completion and the expert receives documented credit.
