Trust, access, and data handling

Application-level protections

  • Django authentication, session handling, CSRF protection, and password hashing.
  • Forms and ORM-backed writes instead of raw SQL construction in public workflows.
  • Stored request, assignment, and completion history for traceability.

Intentional visibility limits

  • Requesters track their own requests.
  • Experts act on assigned work rather than browsing every request.
  • Workers are restricted by category and Tier 1 manages the broader system.
