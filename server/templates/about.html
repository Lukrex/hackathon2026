About The Platform

A collaborative workspace for community requests, experts, and internal coordinators.

Why This App Exists

When needs are handled through scattered emails, chats, and ad-hoc sheets, teams lose priority, ownership, and history. This app gives requests one shared place where they can be submitted, triaged, assigned, and safely closed.

Where It Helps Most

  • Separating urgent requests from lower-priority work.
  • Splitting workload between super-admins and category-assigned workers.
  • Selecting experts based on skills, languages, and reputation.
  • Tracking ownership, communication, and completion status in one place.

What The System Includes

Instead of a full "AI autopilot", this is a practical workflow where technology accelerates decisions while final ownership stays with the team.

  • Request form: requests with deadline, skills, languages, and requester type.
  • Role-based dashboard: different views for users, experts, workers, and super-admins.
  • Expert recommendations: scoring based on profile data and request content.
  • Request chats: communication tied directly to each request.
  • Karma and help history: completed outcomes build expert reputation.

How Roles Are Structured

Request creators

  • Create requests and track status.
  • Communicate in request chat threads.
  • Mark work as completed once delivered.

Internal team and experts

  • Super-admin sees all requests and manages workers.
  • Workers process only their assigned categories.
  • Experts handle assigned tasks and build reputation through completed help.

Technology Foundation

Django
Web app framework, templates, and authentication
SQLite
Local storage for development
Celery-ready
Optional async jobs and matching automation

Product Principle

The platform is built for fast internal execution. It prioritizes operations over marketing storytelling: who submitted what, who owns it, current status, and whether delivery was confirmed.
